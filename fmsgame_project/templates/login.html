Welcome to our mobile game that's so Alpha it's almost Omega.

The game works as follows:

  1. Go outside, or somewhere your phone can get GPS signal
  2. Open this page (the one you're reading right now) on your Android/iPhone. Basically any phone with GPS and Google Maps.
  3. Click below to Login by using a Google account
  4. Allow the service to get your location.
  5. Look at the map. It should show pins that represent FixMyStreet reports near you that we think might never have been fixed.
  6. Walk, drive or cycle until you are at the site of the nearest pin.
  7. Click on the pin on your map, and open the link.
  8. Select whether the problem is now fixed, not fixed, or whether you can't find it.
  9. Win points and appear on our soon-to-be ready League table.
